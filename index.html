The Darkest Chapter

At 31, many believed this would be Messi's last World Cup. The campaign was chaos from start to finish — a frustrating draw with Iceland, a 3-0 humiliation by Croatia that left him visibly broken, then a dramatic late winner over Nigeria to scrape through the group. It all ended with a breathless 4-3 Round of 16 defeat to a teenage Kylian Mbappé and France. The pixelated jersey of 2018 became a symbol of a fractured era.
